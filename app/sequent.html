Sequent

Sequent is an iOS puzzle game. Your goal is to produce specific colors by growing them.

Designed for both iPhone and iPad, Sequent is an amazing chill-out game which can be played on your schedule. There are NO in-app purchases.

We also offer Sequent Immersion (also on the App Store), an edition which contains a full selection of music by several talented independent musicians.

Aside from the music, both editions are exactly the same.

How To Play

Every game begins with a single, random color tile in a sea of empty (black) tiles.

Touch and hold a color tile to harvest it.

Harvesting a color splits it into its red, green, blue components. These appear in the color store - a window at the top right of the screen. You may collect as many color tiles as you want, but the maximum value for any color is 255.

Touch and hold an empty (black) tile to plant the current color you hold.

Planted colors will quickly grow into adjacent tiles. They'll mix with nearby colors and rarely you'll see a minor genetic mutation.

Each action earns you Knowledge - which improves harvest and planting speeds, mutation chances, as well as how many exact copies you'll spawn with each planting.

Tap a tile to place or remove a void tile.

Nothing will grow on or through a void tile. Once you achieve pure red, you can make a compartment for safely growing pure red tiles.

Tips & Tricks

  1. RGB values may be within ten digits for secondary and higher colors, so you don't need to get the exact value.
  2. Planting only consumes up to 255 from each color. If you've gathered more, subtraction may help produce a new shade.
  3. Shoot for pure primary colors first - they're easier to build new colors with.
  4. Use void tiles to keep pure colors safe. This will be even more important for upcoming features.
  5. Colors won't grow if they'll be too dark.
