Helion3 is a web, desktop, and mobile software development company in Portland, Oregon USA. Our work is play, and our playground is a wide range of commercial and open-source projects.

On the web, we've founded pste.me and snowy-evening.com.

On iOS, we've launched Sequent and have several projects in progress.

The Minecraft community knows us as the creators of Prism, DarkMythos, Oracle, Darmok, Elixr, and a few others.

Over twenty-seven thousand people from all over planet Earth have played with us on DHMC.

We have a few game prototypes like Lifeform.

Web development professionals know us as the creators of simpleDOM, Aspen Framework, Peregrine, Form Builder, and other random trinkets.

Clients previously worked with our sister company Botsko.net
