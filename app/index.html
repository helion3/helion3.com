Helion3 is a web, desktop, and mobile software development company in Portland, Oregon USA.

Our work is play, and our playground is a wide spectrum of commercial and open-source projects. We made pste.me and snowy-evening.com.

In the gaming universe we're known for creating Lifeform. The Minecraft community knows us as the creators of Prism, DarkMythos, Oracle, Darmok, Elixr, and a few others.

Over twenty thousand people from all over planet Earth have played with us on DHMC.

Web development professionals know us as the creators of Aspen Framework, Peregrine, the jQuery Form Builder, and other random trinkets.
